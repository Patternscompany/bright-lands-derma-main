    BrightLands Skin, Hair & Laser Clinic is much more than just a treatment center – it is a space where science meets care. With advanced dermatology solutions, we ensure every patient receives the best-in-class treatment in a comfortable and friendly environment.

    Our clinic is led by Dr. Sania Riaz (MBBS, MD DVL), a Consultant Dermatologist, Cosmetologist & Trichologist. With her expertise and passion for skin health, she has helped countless individuals achieve glowing skin, healthy hair, and renewed confidence. Recognized with multiple awards, Dr. Sania is committed to delivering safe, result-oriented treatments that truly make a difference.
